Inexor

Once upon a time Inexor was developed by a headless group of passionate game developers. It was made from gamers for gamers.
It started as a rebellion against the everlasting dictationship of stubborn men; men who disown feature requests and fear the way of progress. Willing to create a game that is 100% open source with 100% open source content, these brave knights fought against a code that could not be worse in terms of design patterns, flexibility, maintainability and structure.
